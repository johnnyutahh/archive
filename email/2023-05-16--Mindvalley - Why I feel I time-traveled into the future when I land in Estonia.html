I recently returned home to Tallinn, Estonia after weeks of traveling.

And as weird as this sounds, it always feels like I just time-traveled into the future the moment I land.

Because this small, unassuming Baltic nation may be one of the most (surprisingly) technologically advanced countries in the world.

So it's no surprise that this is the location of Mindvalley University 2023 where 2,000+ of our members will relocate for up to three weeks to plunge into 300+ Mindvalley events between July 3 and July 23. 

You see, while Mindvalley University brings together one of the most diverse groups of people from all walks of life and of all ages…

There’s a large subset of our community that are brilliant entrepreneurs, digital nomads, and business owners.

All of whom are all looking to level up in their respective fields and expand their network.

If that describes you, Tallinn is the city you’ll want to immerse yourself in.

It has the highest number of startups per capita.

Many of its politicians are young, progressive leaders.

And its incredible coworking infrastructure is perfect for the growing number of people who are embracing remote work.

But there’s also one hidden advantage to Tallinn most people don’t realize. 

And it is its size.

It’s large enough for you to explore and soak in the rich history and culture of its medieval city, and get truly inspired.

But it’s also the right size for those serendipitous encounters with other Mindvalley University attendees – who may end up being your next business partner, co-collaborator and closest ally.

If this is something you’d be excited to be a part of, just know that we’ve also created plenty of other opportunities to connect through community-led masterminding, coaching and workshops.
